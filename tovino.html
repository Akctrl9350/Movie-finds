  • Arm is an upcoming Malayalam movie. The movie is directed by Jithin Lal and will feature Tovino Thomas, Krithi Shetty, Aishwarya Rajesh, and Surabhi Lakshmi as lead characters. Other popular actors who were roped in for Arm are Basil Joseph, Rohini, Harish Uthaman, Jagadish, and Pramod Shetty

ARM is written by Sujith Nambiar and produced by Dr. Zachariah Thomas and Listin Stephen under Magic Frames and UGM Productions banners.
